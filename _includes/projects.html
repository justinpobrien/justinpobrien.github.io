Projects

A few things I'm working on.

Bitcoin Bootcamp - A fast-paced technical course designed to teach students how Bitcoin works under the hood. Focuses on primitives such as digital signatures, cryptographic hashing, Merkle trees, and Bloom filters among others.
Proof of Clairvoyance - A method to commit to a prediction in the blockchain by publishing the root of a Merkle tree in a Bitcoin transaction. Leverages the use of Shamir's secret-sharing scheme, digital signatures and Merkle proofs to validate that each share of the prediction is a node within a securely timestamped Merkle tree.
