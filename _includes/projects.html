Proof of Clairvoyance - A method to commit to a prediction in the blockchain by publishing the root of a Merkle tree in a Bitcoin transaction. Leverages the use of Shamir's secret-sharing scheme, digital signatures and Merkle proofs to validate that subsequent revelations of each share of the prediction are provably part of the initial commitment.
